About Daily Meditation

This app is designed to help you find a moment of calm in your day.

How to Use:

  • Choose a theme to change the background and ambiance.
  • Select a calming sound to help you focus.
  • Set your desired meditation time and press "Start".
  • Follow the breathing animation to guide your breath.
  • Click "New Affirmation" to get a new positive thought.
  • Enter "Zen Mode" to focus solely on the breathing animation.
